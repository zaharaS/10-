The production of tapestry by hand is creativity. Like handmade carpets, every product, even made according to the same layout, has its own peculiarities and differences.

MANUAL TISSUE/THREAD/WOOL

In the process of exploitation, the tapestries are unpretentious. It is enough once a year to wipe with a brush or napkin from dust, you can vacuum it with a little power.

If you’d like to order an art piece, send me an e-mail with the following details:

1. Category (portrait, landscape, postcard)

2. Technique (watercolors, pastel, acrylic, oil)

3. Desired size

4. In case you need a painting of a photo or other existing image, please attach it in high resolution, .jpeg

5. Any additional ideas and comments you might have

After we define the details of your order, you will receive a confirmation document containing the information on the final price of your order, it’s payment details, and the day it will be shipped. I will start working on your order as soon as you confirm it.
